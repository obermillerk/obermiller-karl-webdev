    Dogs Better than Humans, Common Sense Says

    Multiple surveys consisting of random samples collected from areas of abnormally high concentration of certain viewpoints (specifically around dog parks) show that public opinion leans towards dogs being better than people. Scientific findings have been updated to proved this on a biological front, and politicians have agreed to raise awareness of these findings for a modest fee.

    Many participants of the surveyed were dog owners, several claiming to be members of the American Kenel Club.
